Hand Made in Small Batches

Our perfumes are crafted and mixed by hand in small quantities. We select the premium quality perfume materials from all around the world, and exclusively include borneo premium scent ingredients ie. sabah agarwood(oud) , patchouli, vetiver and ambergris.
